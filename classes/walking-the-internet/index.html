In this 3 week class, we will meet three consecutive Saturday afternooons in July to explore physical infrastructure in New York City. How does one “see” the internet, anyway? We will explore how the internet is not some ethereal, untouchable thing, but physically rooted — connected by underwater submarine cables and served from data centers containing many literal computers worldwide. This class is taught by Alex Wolfe and Laurel Schwulst and takes place in different places in Manhattan each week.

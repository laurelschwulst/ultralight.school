September 20, 2025 /

TWA: Total Work of Art (Looking Back)

Our class called “TWA” or “Total Work of Art” or “Thinking with Air” or “Three Worlds Aligned” happened this summer in June, July, and August, meeting twice a week in East Williamsburg, Brooklyn, NY...

Ten participants arrived with their own “points of departure” or “idea for a single thing” and gradually transformed it into distinct digital, physical, and temporal forms, creating new worlds...

After seven weeks of explorations, the class culminated in a final “TWA BBQ,” where a barbecue gradually merged into participant temporal encounters, including: board meetings, launch parties, movement exercises, product demos, screenprinting, readings, and live or interactive installations...

Thanks to all the participants including Maddy, Ha, Mary, Halim, Siri, Quinlan, Ritam, Jiamin, Maria, and Goda; teachers Bryant, Stephen, and Laurel; and telos for hosting!

Special thanks to Vincent for BBQ grilling; Matthew for the photos; and our guest lecturers Spencer, Nile, and Salome!

As Min Guhong Manufacturing says, “If the concept is fun or interesting, it becomes an engine or machine. It keeps producing interesting stuff, almost by itself…”

September 20, 2025 /

Sense to Sense (Looking Back)

Our sensorial writing and publication class, “Sense to Sense,” happened in June and July for five weeks, exploring the five senses....

Twelve participants met on Sunday and Monday evenings to discuss carrying sensations into written form. By the end, we created a publication... which is available to purchase, while supplies last! ($15 + $5 USD shipping! US domestic orders only, thanks.)

On Friday, August 1st, we hosted a launch party inside an elegant skyscraper lobby called Park Avenue Plaza in midtown — with a piano, some real and fake greenery, and a waterfall. It's a POPS (Privately Owned Public Space), so it was free for us to use...

There were some readings, refreshments, and a fragrance bar. Thanks to our participants, including: Ariana, Nat, Jess, Kara, Alicia, Hannah, alphonse, Tiffany, Miaoye, Kim, Jill, and Hannah; our teachers Meg and Laurel; and our Monday classroom at Dean's List in Boerum Hill, Brooklyn...

Thanks everyone for the amazing class and publication!

Ultralight School celebrates Summer 2025!

Hi everyone,

Greetings from Ultralight School!

Our first season has drawn to a close, and we are celebrating the culmination of a summer of special classes and activities. Thanks for joining us, whether in-person or in-spirit or both...

***

This summer, we held three different classes —

( Class 1 of 3 — Sense to Sense )

Our five week writing and publication class, “Sense to Sense,” happened between June and July for five weeks, to cover the five senses....

You can purchase the publication that came out of “Sense to Sense” while supplies last. It is available for $15 + $5 USD shipping! US domestic orders only, thanks.

( Class 2 of 3 — TWA )

Our class called “TWA” or “Total Work of Art” or “Thinking with Air” happened in June, July, and the beginning of August. Ten participants arrived with their own “points of departure” and expressed their ideas through digital, physical, and temporal forms. The class met on Thursday evenings and Sunday afternoons for seven weeks and culminated in a final “TWA BBQ” or “TWA Barbecue.” Thanks to all the participants; teachers Bryant, Stephen, and Laurel; and telos for hosting! And special thanks to our guests Spencer Doran, Nile Koetting, and Salome Oggenfuss. We will be sharing a handful of more photos from TWA soon!

Announcing “TWA”

Hi everyone,

We’re excited to launch our class called “TWA” —

In “TWA: Total Work of Art,” we invite artists, designers, writers, programmers, and anyone else interested to shapeshift their concept across three modalities: 1) digital, 2) physical, and 3) temporal. Inspired by wide-ranging examples of design as world-building — think Bauhaus, Beyoncé, Bernadette Corporation — this workshop responds to how culture is experienced today, inviting participants to develop a core concept across multiple forms and points of access.

This 7 week course is led by us, three teachers: Stephen Kwok, Laurel Schwulst, and Bryant Wells. Meeting twice a week (on Thursdays and Sundays) in-person in Brooklyn, each session includes conversational lectures, technical demonstrations, presentation and critique time, and guest visits. The course culminates in a public event in which the participants present the totality of their work.

We’re launching the class under “Ultralight School,” a new educational umbrella, beginning this summer. It’s a natural continuation of Laurel’s previous “Fruitful School” with a related ethos but new summertime levity 🍃

We invite you to apply to TWA here. Applications are due 11:59pm EST on May 30, 2025.

Write us twa@ultralight.school if you have any questions.

Looking forward to seeing you this summer!

Stephen Kwok, Laurel Schwulst, & Bryant Wells
